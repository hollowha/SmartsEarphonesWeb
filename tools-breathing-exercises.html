Box Breathing Technique

Discover how to use box breathing to manage stress effectively. This technique involves inhaling, holding, exhaling, and pausing for four seconds each. Practice along with guided audio through your SmartSense Earphones.

4-7-8 Breathing Pattern

This breathing pattern aims to reduce anxiety or help people get to sleep. The pattern works by inhaling for 4 seconds, holding the breath for 7 seconds, and exhaling for 8 seconds.

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Learn to relax your muscles through a two-step process that helps alleviate stress. This technique teaches you to systematically tense and then relax different muscle groups in your body.

Mindful Breathing

Focus on your breathing and be present. Mindful breathing can help you manage your reactions to stress and anxiety and is an excellent tool for relaxation.
